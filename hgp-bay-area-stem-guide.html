Berkeley Lab Undergraduate & Diverse Pathways (BLDAP)
Free
Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory

Project-based STEM program for grades 10–11. Build 21st-century skills, learn about real research at Berkeley Lab, and get college and career guidance from working scientists. Applications open Feb 2, 2026.

SAGE Summer Camp @ Berkeley Lab
Free
Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory

A one-week summer camp for public high school students ages 14–17. Job shadowing, hands-on projects, and professional development alongside actual STEM professionals at a world-class research facility.

Multi-STEM Life Sciences
Ages 14–17 Berkeley Summer

Paid Stipend
Joint BioEnergy Institute (JBEI) · Berkeley Lab

A paid summer science intensive for high school students. Deepen your understanding of biotechnology, microbiology, and biofuels at a cutting-edge research institute. Applications open in early spring.

Biology Bioengineering
High School Emeryville / Berkeley Summer

COSMOS – California Summer School for Mathematics & Science
Fee (Aid Available)
University of California System

California's premier state-sponsored STEM summer residential program for grades 8–12. Hosted at UC campuses including UC Davis and UC Santa Cruz — intense coursework, real labs, and a community of peers who love STEM as much as you do.

Math Science Engineering CS
Grades 8–12 UC Campuses (NorCal) Summer (Residential)
cosmos-ucop.ucdavis.edu Learn More →
SMASH Academy – Summer Math & Science Honors
Free
Level Playing Field Institute · Oakland

A free, rigorous 5-week summer residential STEM program for underrepresented students at UC Berkeley. Students complete college-prep coursework in math, science, and technology. One of the most respected programs in the Bay Area — former HGP alumni have attended.

Math Science Technology
Grades 9–12 UC Berkeley Summer (Residential)

Primer on computing fundamentals, hands-on quantum physics, and applying quantum phenomena to solve computing problems. You don't need to be an expert — curiosity is the only prerequisite.
