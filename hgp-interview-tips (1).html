The Hidden Genius Project · Your Guide to Show Up & Stand Out

💡

This is your moment to be real, be you, and be ready. The Hidden Genius Project isn't just looking for the "perfect" student — they're looking for the genuine you. Here's how to bring your best self to Interview Day.

// Interview Day Tips
01
🎯
Know & Own Your "Why"

Before Interview Day, sit down and write out why you actually want to be in this program — not what sounds good, but what's true for you. Do you want to build apps? Start a business? Learn skills your school doesn't teach? Be around people who push you? Whatever it is, own it and say it with confidence. Interviewers can tell the difference between a real answer and one that's just trying to impress. Your authentic "why" is your superpower.

02
👂
Listen Before You Speak

In a group activity, pause and hear what others are saying before jumping in. Strong leaders listen first — it shows you're thoughtful and respect your teammates.

03
🙋🏾
Speak Up, But Don't Dominate

You need to contribute — silence won't get you in. Share your ideas clearly. But also make space for others. Being a team player is just as impressive as being vocal.

04
👀
Body Language Talks Too

Sit up. Make eye contact. Put the phone away. How you carry yourself tells a story before you even open your mouth. Look engaged even when you're not the one speaking.

05
❓
It's Okay to Ask Questions

If you don't understand an activity or prompt, ask for clarification. Asking smart questions shows curiosity and confidence — not weakness.

06
🔄
Reset Between Rotations

Each station is a fresh start. If one round didn't go great, shake it off. Take a breath, re-center, and bring full energy to the next one. Resilience is a genius trait.

07
🤝
Build Up, Don't Shut Down

If a teammate shares an idea, build on it — even if you have a better one. Say "Yes, and..." instead of "No, that won't work." Collaboration is a core HGP value.

08
✅
Be Prepared & On Time

Arrive early. Know the schedule. Interview Day runs 9AM–1PM with rotating sessions. Being punctual and prepared shows you take the opportunity seriously.

Express Your "Why"

// The most important thing you'll say all day

At some point during the interview, you'll have a chance to share why you genuinely want to join The Hidden Genius Project. Don't overthink it — just be honest. Use these prompts to help you prepare:

"When I think about the future I want, I imagine..."
"I want to join HGP because something in me is tired of..."
"The skill I most want to learn or develop is..."
"My community needs people who can..."
✍🏾 Write your answer out before Interview Day
// Do's & Don'ts
✅ DO
Introduce yourself with confidence
Share your real experiences and goals
Encourage teammates during group tasks
Stay focused during every station

Be yourself — fully and unapologetically
❌ DON'T
